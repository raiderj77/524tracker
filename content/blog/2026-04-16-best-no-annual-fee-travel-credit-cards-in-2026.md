### How do No Annual Fee Travel Credit Cards Work?

No annual fee travel credit cards, also known as cashback and travel cards, provide rewards and benefits similar to those of premium cards, but without charging an annual fee. ## Frequently Asked Questions
**Q: Can I use these credit cards for international travel?**
A: Most no annual fee travel credit cards have no foreign transaction fee, making them ideal for international trips. However, check the terms and conditions to confirm no additional fees apply.

**Q: Are these cards open to everyone?**
A: Eligibility requirements may vary for no annual fee travel credit cards. Some cards have specific income or credit score requirements, so check the card details before applying.

**Q: How do I redeem my points?**
A: You can usually redeem points for travel purchases by phone or online through the credit card issuer's website or mobile app. Follow the issuer's redemption guidelines to maximize your rewards.

**Q: Can I transfer my rewards to another credit card or loyalty program?**
A: Check the credit card terms and conditions to see if you can transfer rewards to another credit card or loyalty program.
