## Where to Sign Up

- **Chase**: No direct affiliate program for most cards. Use CardRatings or NerdWallet affiliate networks, or CJ Affiliate.
- **American Express**: Direct affiliate program available via CJ Affiliate (cj.com). Amex also has a direct referral program.
- **Capital One**: Direct affiliate program available. Also available through CJ Affiliate.
- **Citi**: Available through CJ Affiliate and FlexOffers.
- **General**: Most credit card affiliate programs are available through:
  - CJ Affiliate (cj.com)
  - FlexOffers (flexoffers.com)
  - Impact (impact.com)
  - Direct issuer portals

## Notes

- All affiliate links use `rel="nofollow sponsored noopener noreferrer"` and `target="_blank"`
- Each link has `data-affiliate-card` and `data-affiliate-bank` attributes for analytics
- Affiliate disclosure is visible above the fold on the top-cards page and before the card section on the homepage
