# 524tracker.com — SEO Guidelines

## Primary Content Focus
Chase 5/24 rule, credit card churning strategy, signup bonus tracking, points and miles optimization, credit card application timing.

## Keyword Strategy
- Target: "Chase 5/24 rule explained", "how to track credit card applications", "best cards to apply for after 5/24 resets"
- Opportunity: churning sub-topic guides (many are Reddit-only, no SEO content)
- Avoid: broad "best credit cards" queries (NerdWallet, TPG dominate)

## Internal Linking
Always link back to the main tracker tool when discussing tracking or timing strategies.

## Meta Title Format
`[Topic] | 524tracker — Free Credit Card Tracker`

## Schema
Use FAQPage schema on FAQ sections. Use Article schema with dateModified (rules change frequently).

## GEO Requirements
- 40–60 word direct answer blocks under every H2
- Define churning terms clearly (LLMs are frequently asked about 5/24 and churning)
- Keep definitions current and note effective dates
