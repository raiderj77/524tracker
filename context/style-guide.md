# 524tracker.com — Style Guide

## Formatting
- Use tables for card comparisons (Markdown tables)
- Use numbered lists for application sequencing strategies
- Bold card names on first mention in each section
- Always note "as of [date]" for any rule or bonus amount that may change

## Terminology
- "5/24" not "five twenty-four" (the community uses the shorthand)
- "churning" is acceptable and understood by the audience
- "SUB" = signup bonus (acceptable abbreviation with audience)
- "MSR" = minimum spending requirement
- "PC" = product change (card upgrade/downgrade without new application)
- "DP" = data point (community term for user-reported evidence)

## Disclaimer (Required on Every Article)
*This is for informational purposes only and does not constitute financial advice. Credit card terms, bonuses, and rules change frequently. Always verify current offers directly with the card issuer before applying.*

## CTA Style
- "Track your applications → 524tracker"
- "See when your 5/24 clock resets → [tool link]"
