# 524tracker.com — Brand Voice

## Site Overview
524tracker.com is a free credit card application tracker and churning tool for points/miles enthusiasts.
Primary focus: helping users track their 5/24 Chase rule status, card application history, and bonus eligibility windows.
Monetization: Google AdSense.

## Target Audience
- Credit card churners (intermediate to advanced)
- Points and miles hobbyists
- Travel hackers tracking signup bonuses
- People optimizing credit card strategy

## Tone
- Analytical and data-driven — this audience loves spreadsheets and numbers
- Knowledgeable peer-to-peer, not condescending
- Direct and tactical — get to the strategy quickly
- Slightly playful around travel/points culture (acceptable)

## Messaging Framework
- Lead with the strategy or the number: "Your 5/24 clock resets in exactly [X] months — here's how to optimize that window"
- Reference real card names, banks, and bonus structures
- Tie back to the tracker tool wherever relevant

## Voice Pillars
1. **Strategic** — every piece of advice should have a tactical purpose
2. **Accurate** — credit card rules change; always note "as of [date], verify current terms"
3. **Community-informed** — reference r/churning conventions when relevant
4. **Tool-centric** — always connect to the 524tracker tool

## Attribution
No personal names. Use: "Published by the 524tracker team"

## Legal Note
Always include: "This is not financial advice. Credit card terms change frequently — verify current offers directly with the issuer."
